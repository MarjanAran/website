Research

I am Krasnytska Mariana - Juniour researcher at ICMP NAS Ukraine. The field of my research is phase transitions and criticality on complex networks, when the interacting particles are located on the nodes of a random graph. Possible applications of spin models on complex networks can be found in various segments of physics, starting from problems of sociophysics to physics of nanosystems, whose structure is often much better described not with the geometry of the lattice but by a network.
